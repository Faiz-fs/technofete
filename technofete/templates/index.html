Technofete’23 - Intra College Technical Symposium

It is a much-anticipated event exclusively designed for the talented students of MCET. This symposium is scheduled to unfold from the 6th to the 8th of November 2023, promising an exciting and enriching experience.

Technofete’23 represents an intra-college extravaganza where the Students' Guild of Service (SGS) collaborates closely with the Students Research Council (SRC) and various Department Associationsto curate a diverse array of events. These events are meticulously crafted to empower our students, allowing them to participate actively and demonstrate their exceptional talents, be it in the realm of technology, management, or other fields. It's a grand platform for students to unleash their creativity, innovation, and expertise. We look forward to witnessing an inspiring showcase of brilliance at Technofete’23
